A Few Words About Us

Aqua Tista We have established in the Year 2014 started by Mr.V.S Krishna Anand We are the Private owned company serving in bangalore,Our Mission is to deliver pure water at the tap of a button.

Potable water being one of the most critical needs of mankind, next to oxygen, triggered our journey to deliver pure & hygienic drinking water at the doorstep of every Bangalore resident at an affordable price, quality & timely service. Though water supply from local government is available in certain parts of Bangalore, it is generally not considered safe enough to drink until it undergoes several filtering & processing steps.
